Shipping & Delivery

Packaging

Every artwork is professionally packaged to ensure maximum protection during transit. Paintings are wrapped in anti-scratch paper, foam padding and a rigid outer container. All packages are labelled "Fragile – Artwork".

Insurance

All shipments are insured for the full value of the artwork. If your order arrives damaged, please refer to the Return & Refund Policy for instructions.
