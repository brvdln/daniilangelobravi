Contact data: name, email address, content of the message sent through the contact form.

Visit statistics

This site uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly visit statistics service that does not use cookies and does not collect identifying data of individual visitors (no fingerprinting, no data retained beyond the aggregate statistical function). The following are recorded in aggregate form: pages visited, country of origin, generic device/browser, and referring site. For more information: GoatCounter Privacy Policy.

Site hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc. (USA). GitHub may collect technical browsing data (IP address, user agent, request times) necessary to provide the hosting service. For more information: GitHub Privacy Statement.

Payment management

Artwork purchases are processed through Stripe, a third-party payment service (Stripe Payments Europe, Ltd.). At the time of payment you will be redirected to a secure checkout page hosted by Stripe: name, shipping address and card/payment method details are collected and processed directly by Stripe according to the highest industry security standards (PCI-DSS), without ever passing through this site. Daniil Angelo Bravi receives from Stripe only the information necessary to fulfill the order (name, shipping address, amount). For more information: Stripe Privacy Policy.

Contact form

The form on the Contact page is managed by Formsubmit.co, a third-party service that receives and forwards by email the data entered (name, email address, message) for the sole purpose of responding to the request. Formsubmit.co is not used for profiling or marketing purposes. For more information: Formsubmit.co Privacy Policy.
