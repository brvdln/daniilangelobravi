Valuation Method
Width × Height × €3/cm²

Every work starts from a price based on the surface of the canvas: width times height, multiplied by a coefficient of €3 per square centimetre. This is an indicative value, later adjusted according to the complexity of the work. Frame and shipping are counted separately.
