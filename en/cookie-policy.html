Cookie Policy

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and the choices available to users regarding their use.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that visited websites send to the user's device, where they are stored and then retransmitted to the same websites during subsequent visits.

This site does not use cookies

The site is built with static HTML/CSS/JavaScript and does not install any cookie of its own (no login, no cart, no session to remember).

Visit statistics — no cookies

This site uses GoatCounter to collect visit statistics in aggregate and anonymous form. GoatCounter does not install any cookie and does not perform device fingerprinting: for this reason, visit tracking does not require consent under cookie regulations. More information in the Privacy Policy.

Profiling cookies

This site does not use profiling cookies or targeted advertising.

YouTube videos — privacy-enhanced mode

The videos embedded in the 3D page use the youtube-nocookie.com domain: YouTube does not install tracking cookies when the page loads. By clicking play to start playback, YouTube (Google Ireland Ltd.) may install technical cookies necessary for the video's playback. More information: Google Cookie Policy.
