Return & Refund Policy

Nature of the artworks

Each painting sold through this site is an original, one-of-a-kind work, hand-painted by Daniil Angelo Bravi. As these are not mass-produced products, the conditions described below apply.

Returns for change of mind

Returns for change of mind are not accepted once a purchase is confirmed, given the unique and original nature of each artwork.

Damage during shipping

If the artwork arrives damaged during shipping, contact me within 7 days of delivery through the Contact page, attaching clear photos of the damage and the packaging. I will assess a full or partial refund, or — if possible — a replacement, depending on the extent of the damage.

Refunds

If a refund is approved, the amount will be returned via the same payment method used for the purchase (Stripe), within 14 business days of approval.

How to request a return or refund

Write to me through the Contact page, including the name of the purchased artwork, the order date, and the reason for the request.

Courses and lessons

Video courses and in-person lessons are not covered by this return policy; any requests related to courses should be arranged directly through the Contact page.
