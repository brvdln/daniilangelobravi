Bio

As a child he loved to draw and color: today he is a painter and draughtsman, as well as an industrial designer in the footwear industry, with over 8 years of professional experience in mold design and construction and 3D modeling with Rhinoceros for the footwear and accessories fashion industry. Graduated in Industrial and Environmental Design from the University of Camerino.
